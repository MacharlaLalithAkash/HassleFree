What do we do?

Medical shop management is not an easy task. Coming to the present scenario in the global pandemic covid19, pharmacy management has become much more difficult.

Under these consequences, we "HasselFree" sharpen our mind to get the new pharmacy management system with digital technology.

You can locate the medicines in your pharmacy, check the stock and restock if required. By this, you can reduce the manpower in your pharmacy and save your money.

Locate Medicines

Locate any medicine in the stock precisely without moving away from the desk.

Generate Bill

Bill the medicines automatically, save the bill digitally and calculate your daily profits.

Refill Stock

Get notified when a refill is required. Generate a report of stock sold every day.

Monthly Report

Generate report every month automatically. Manage and increase your profits.

Customer Info

Store customer's information digitally for 30 days to maintain intigrity. And improve the trust.

Expiry Notifications

Inputing the expiry dates of stocked medicines and gives an alert when a particular is expired.
